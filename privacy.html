For example, a cookie can be used to collect information about a user's online surfing history when they visit a certain website. Other technologies, such as cookies, may allow a Web user to retrieve previously input Personal Information from a server.

Cookie management is generally available in most browsers, allowing you to choose whether or not to accept cookies, as well as how to delete them. You can learn more about cookie management here. Most browsers allow you to choose whether or not to receive cookies, as well as whether or not to be notified when a cookie is received.

It is possible that certain portions of the Web site will not function properly if you choose to block your cookies; in this case, you will have to enter your password and user ID again.
